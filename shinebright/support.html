Common questions

  • Where's my quote? Requests are answered personally — you'll get a push notification the moment it's ready. Check the Chat tab for the offer card.
  • How do I pay? Zelle from the visit's payment screen (upload your transfer screenshot), or cash/card in person when we finish.
  • How do coupons work? One coupon per visit, applied in the booking flow when you book. No cash value; expiration dates apply.
  • How do I delete my account? Profile → delete account. Your personal data is removed right away (details in the Privacy Policy).
