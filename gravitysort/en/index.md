# Privacy Policy — Gravity Sort

**Last updated:** May 6, 2026

Thank you for playing Gravity Sort ("the app", "the game"). This policy describes what data we collect and how we use it.

> 🇲🇽 [Español (canonical)](../) · 🇵🇹 [Português](../pt/) · 🇫🇷 [Français](../fr/) · 🇩🇪 [Deutsch](../de/) · 🇯🇵 [日本語](../ja/)

## 1. Data we collect

### 1.1 Game data (local)
- Level progress, stars, coins, streak, unlocked achievements
- Settings (sound, vibration, colorblind mode, language, notifications)
- Unlocked / applied skins
- Active In-App Purchases (Coin Doubler, Rescue Pack, etc.)

This data is stored **on your device** via UserDefaults and, optionally, synced to your personal iCloud account (NSUbiquitousKeyValueStore + private CloudKit). **We never see this data** — it lives in your Apple ID.

### 1.2 Advertising data (Google AdMob)
If you grant consent via Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt, we may share your **Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA)** with Google AdMob for personalized ads. Without consent, ads are contextual (no IDFA shared).

AdMob may also collect:
- Approximate IP address (fraud prevention)
- Ad interaction events (impressions, clicks, completions)
- App diagnostic data (ad-related crashes)

Full Google policy: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

If we publish in EU/UK/Switzerland, we show a GDPR consent form (Google UMP) before the ATT prompt. You can change your consent at any time from **Settings → Privacy options**.

### 1.3 In-App Purchases (StoreKit)
Purchases are processed via Apple App Store. We receive only purchase confirmation (transaction receipt); we do not see your payment data. For refunds: reportaproblem.apple.com

### 1.4 Performance diagnostics
We use Apple's native MetricKit to detect crashes and performance issues. Data is stored locally and is NOT sent to any third-party server.

## 2. Data we do NOT collect
- Email, name, phone
- Location (precise or manual approximate geolocation)
- Contact list
- Photos / camera / microphone access
- Any directly identifiable personal data

## 3. Accounts and data deletion
Gravity Sort **does not require a user account**. All data is on your device or your personal iCloud.

To delete all data:
1. Delete the app from your device (clears local UserDefaults)
2. **iOS Settings → [Your name] → iCloud → Manage Storage → Gravity Sort → Delete Data** (clears iCloud)
3. If you bought IAPs, entitlements remain in your Apple ID — restorable on any reinstall
